Broadwand

At my previous job, I had the pleasure of participating as the lead designer on a project that strived to bring more afforable internet to people. The pilot program was in the country of Colombia with a strong carrier as our partners.

The project consists of a suite of Android apps that monitor your celular data usage and calculates the price of any given webpage or content.
