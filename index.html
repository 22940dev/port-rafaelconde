Frames

I've built this little iPhone app that allows you to mirror, store offline and browse through all of your Framer prototypes.
It was the most challenging design process that I've endured, lot of self imposed restraints and personal goals.
Available for free in the App Store.

SketchCasts

Three to four times a month I record a screencast covering my favourite design tool – Sketch. I’ve been doing this for over a year, and I charge 8 dollars a month for a membership.
Recently expanded and dip my toes on Framer.

the ARTBOARD

Me and my pal CJ Melegrito started doing this podcast thing, where we get the chance to every now and then talk to some awesome fellow designers have fun discussions.

iOS 8 UI Kit +

Back when iOS 8 was unveiled at WWDC, I immediately started recreating every native element on Sketch, every app icon on the springboard, share sheets, you name it.
Free download on Github.
