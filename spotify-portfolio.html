The addition of the background blur effect looks nice, no doubt, but it’s a very obvious wink at Rdio. There’s no need for that.
Let’s build a strong brand identity, an original one.

Let's be diferent

Every music player on a mobile platform looks exactly the same. Navigation on top, album art, name of the artist/song and controls.
I’m not showing you the best way, I’m just looking for another way.

Discovery

You probably know this better than anyone – music discovery is a tricky thing to get right. There’s basically two kinds of music listeners: there’s the, lets call him normal listener, the one that wants to listen to the songs he knows, what he heard on last night’s The Voice, be surprised by an all time classic every now and then. And then there’s the hipster listener, the trend setter, the kind of listener that wants to discover new music, all the time, he wants to be surprised, he reads Pitchfork and leaves reviews on Twitter or his blog.

Probably there isn’t a perfect system that everyone will like, but boy we can try. I think you guys already have the solution – it’s called Echonest.
